SAFETY & INSURANCE

We believe that two very important factors should govern our operations: SAFETY & SECURITY. We accomplish these goals as follows:

  • We carry our own insurance which totals $5,000,000.
  • We are SYTA, NTA, IATAN, BBB, WPO and ABA members.
  • We only use coach companies with at a minimum of $5,000,000 insurance and who’s bus driver’s are school board approved with certified drivers. A Certificate of Insurance endorsed to your school can be provided upon request.
  • We not only use chain hotels like Marriott, Sheraton, Radisson,  Doubletree and Holiday Inn but we also make sure that your hotel is in a safe neighborhood with interior corridors. Upon request, specific hotels  and beachfront properties can be arranged. If you require, additional  night time security guards exclusive to your group can be arranged at your hotel.

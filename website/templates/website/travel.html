    Four Winds Tours & Travel was established in 1994 for the purpose of creating a memorable experience for students and other groups while ensuring their safety and well-being.

    Each trip is packed with fun and excitement to any destination of your choosing! For over a decade, we continue to specialize in tours for High Schools, Middle and Elementary Schools, as well as Camps, Youth Organizations, Adult Groups, etc. We also customize trips for each department and fit the group’s unique needs, such as performance planning, clinics, workshops, Behind the Scene tours, Space Camp, exclusive events, special meals, handicap accessibility, and any other requests.
